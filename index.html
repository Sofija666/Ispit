About us

Pizzeria was founded in 2010. A small family manufactory with more than a decade of pizza making experience.

In our pizzeria, the most important things are food quality and fast delivery. Our pizzas are prepared with specially selected flour and dough that stands for at least 72 hours in order to achieve the best digestibility. Come or order and see for yourself.
